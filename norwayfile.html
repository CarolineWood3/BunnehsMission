Norway

I may be a bit of a sun-seeker but I'm not afraid to go up North either! And if you like majestic scenery and wide horizons, I can certainly recommend Norway for a visit. Sadly we didn't get to see the Northern Lights but I did get up close to a Polar Bear - just as well it wasn't a real one or I might have ended up being its dinner...
